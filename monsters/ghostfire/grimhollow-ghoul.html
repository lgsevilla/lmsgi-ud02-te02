Ghoul (Grim Hollow)

Medium undead, typically neutral evil

Description

In Grim Hollow, ghouls are the grim end of a cruel bargain—mortals who fed on the dead or were cursed by shadowed powers. They haunt graveyards and warfields, moving with a predator’s patience before erupting into frantic, flesh-tearing savagery. Packs often serve darker masters, trading scraps of sentience for a steady feast.

Quick Stats

Armor Class:
12 (natural armor)
Hit Points:
22 (5d8)
Speed:
30 ft.
Abilities:

Stealth +4, Perception +2
Senses:
Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 12
Damage Immunities:
Poison
Condition Immunities:
Poisoned
Languages:
Understands the languages it knew in life but can’t speak
Challenge:
1 (200 XP)

Traits

Keen Smell. The ghoul has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

Paralyzing Claws. A creature hit by the ghoul’s claws must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be paralyzed for 1 minute. The target can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success. Elves are immune to this effect.
