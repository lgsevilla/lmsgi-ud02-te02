Acolyte

Medium humanoid (any race), any alignment

Description

Acolytes serve in shrines and temples as caretakers and petitioners, calling on their deity for guidance and protection. In the field, they bolster companions with healing prayers and protective blessings.

Quick Stats

Armor Class:
10
Hit Points:
9 (2d8)
Speed:
30 ft.
Abilities:
STR 10 (+0), DEX 10 (+0), CON 10 (+0), INT 10 (+0), WIS 14 (+2), CHA 11 (+0)
Skills:
Medicine +4, Religion +2
Senses:
Passive Perception 12
Languages:
Any one (usually Common)
Challenge:
1/4 (50 XP)

Traits

Spellcasting. The acolyte is a 1st-level spellcaster (WIS is spellcasting ability). Typical prepared spells include bless, cure wounds, and sanctuary.

Found In

This creature appears in The Wild Beyond the Witchlight.
