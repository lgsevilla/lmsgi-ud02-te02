Imp

Tiny fiend (devil), lawful evil

Description

Imps serve as spies, tempters, and messengers for greater devils. They delight in striking from invisibility, sowing paranoia, and offering “help” that nudges mortals toward damnation.

Quick Stats

Armor Class:
13 (natural armor)
Hit Points:
10 (3d4 + 3)
Speed:
20 ft., fly 40 ft.
Abilities:
STR 6 (−2), DEX 17 (+3), CON 13 (+1), INT 11 (+0), WIS 12 (+1), CHA 14 (+2)
Damage Resistances:
Cold; Bludgeoning/Piercing/Slashing from nonmagical, non-silvered attacks
Damage Immunities:
Fire, Poison
Condition Immunities:
Poisoned
Senses:
Darkvision 120 ft., Passive Perception 11
Languages:
Infernal, Common
Challenge:
1 (200 XP)

Traits

Shapechanger. The imp can polymorph into a beastlike form (rat, raven, or spider) while retaining its statistics.

Invisibility. The imp magically turns invisible until it attacks or its concentration ends.

Found In

This creature appears in Baldur’s Gate: Descent into Avernus.
