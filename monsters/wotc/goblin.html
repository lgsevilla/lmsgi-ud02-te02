Goblin

Small humanoid (goblinoid), neutral evil

Description

Goblins are small, green-skinned humanoids known for their cunning, speed, and love for ambush tactics. They thrive in dark caves, ruined strongholds, and tangled forests, attacking in groups with crude weapons and improvised traps. Though cowardly on their own, goblins are dangerous when led by stronger creatures or motivated by greed.
